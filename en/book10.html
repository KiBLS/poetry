Poem 01 - The last passing hour
   

The last passing hour

It was long been written that I'll win.

Hypnotically attractive and yet despised.
Ephemeral and yet forever immortalized.

Just one last awakening, one last strike.
And everything will work the way I like.

Poem 08 - Recognize freedom

Like a regal lion I wanted to roar.

But honestly I became just a simple worker.

In this way the time until now has passed.
But another change will happen very fast.

All burdens and worries are suddenly gone.
After I realized that I've already won.
