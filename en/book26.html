Poem 04 - Gain by loss
   

Gain by loss


Refusal to vaccinate is severely penalized.

On the one hand, there is the biggest loss.

For he who owns nothing owns everything.
And out of a beggar then becomes a king.

Poem 05 - Freeman on his path
   

Freeman on his path


I have no fixed place of residence.
The world is my place of existence.

I don't need an ID card or passport.
Cause the entire world is my resort.

I don't pay any taxes or contributions.
Cause all these are worldly delusions.

I do not use money or a bank account.
Nature provides me a special discount.

I am not registered or reported anywhere,
I have and own only the things that I wear.

I wander to many different places.
And there I leave my personal traces.

My creativity is expressed in the spirit.
And so I write the most intense lyrik.

    (written by KiBLS)

Poem 06 - An endless doctrine
   

An endless doctrine


The noose is slowly being tightened,
and ever more people are frightened.

they thought these times had passed.

Now the time of revelation is drawing near.
And many can already feel a gnawing fear.

The evil will very soon lift its herded veil.
But this time it'll surpass its former scale.

As long as money rules, history will be repeated.
But the day is near when evil will be defeated.

Poem 07 - Choose your words wisely
   

Choose your words wisely


There is good but also bad word magic,
They convey the best or the most tragic.

Everywhere they leave their own traces.

Formed from thoughts, they are effective,

Spells that germinate like many seeds,
and out of them are born the deeds.

Who recognizes the wisdom and its way,
knows about the significance of the array.

Everything was always interconnected.
Through interplay, everyone is affected.

After all, money no longer played a role.
Thus we realized a long forbidden goal.

We all worked together on our well-being.
And we realized our unity as the All-Seeing.

    (written by KiBLS)
