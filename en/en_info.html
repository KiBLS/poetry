Here you will find answers to your questions

General questions
How do I navigate through the website?
Click on the chessboard graphic and you will always be redirected back to the main page.

All poems, books and graphics must be clicked to display them, by clicking them again you can close the respective contribution.

If you have any questions about the navigation, please write to me here
Are all poems your intellectual property?
Yes, I am the author of every poem.

However, some poems were written with co-authors. Nevertheless, they are subject to the same free license and can be reused in the same way.
How can I support you?
- Read more of my poems
- Share my poems with others
- Work on yourself and your consciousness

And of course I'm happy if you subscribe to my website and revisit it from time to time. I am also very pleased about inspiring comments. If you want to send me a gift, you can do it on this page.
Can I link to your website?
It would be great if you would recommend my website to others. Appropriate banners for websites will be added soon.
Are you a Freemason?
No, I don't belong to any organization or club. I associate the chessboard with a personal experience. The same applies to my black sun poetry.
Questions about usage and the license
May I share your poems with others?
It would be great if you would recommend or share my poems to others. You will always find the respective link to each poem below, you just have to click on it once and the respective link will automatically be copied to your clipboard. The same happens when you click on a poem.

You can also share all english poem graphics. They already contain all the necessary license information. So feel free to distribute them.
So what do I need to specify to share your poems?
You must always indicate the author, title, license and source under each poem. If you do this, you are doing everything right and can use my poems as you want and where you want.
Questions about the audio books and files
Who speaks your audio books and audio files?
All my audio books were spoken by an AI.

For more information visit this website https://ttsfree.com/.
Can I download individual poems as audio?
Yes, you will find a download option under each poem to download it in TXT, PNG or MP3 format.

Just select the MP3 format to download the respective poem as audio file.
Questions about the poem graphics
May I share your poem graphics?
It would be great if you would recommend or share my poems graphics to others. You will always find the respective links to each poem graphic below, you just have to click on it once and the respective link will automatically be copied to your clipboard.

They already contain all the necessary license information. So feel free to distribute them.

Questions about the 3D graphics
Which software was used to create the 3D graphics?
Each 3D graphic was created by me with Blender 3D.

For more information visit this website https://www.blender.org/.
Can I use your 3D graphics?
Under each 3D graphic are also the license conditions noted. Usually my 3D graphics are as free as my poems, but there are exceptions. If no license condition is noted under the 3D graphic, you are not allowed to use the graphic. But there are only a few exceptions.
Can you send me a .blend file?
No, not anymore!
Questions about the random poems
The random generator does not work?
For the random number generator to work, Javascript must be enabled in the browser.
After 10 or 15 clicks I got a double poem?
The category Random Poems works with Javascript and a random generator. It is possible that duplicate poems are displayed from time to time. The english version contains 159 poem graphics which are displayed randomly.

Who or what is KiBLS?
KiBLS is a kind of acronym and at the same time a newly formed artificial word. KiBLS started writing poetry privately in 2007.

On 10.04.2017 KiBLS began to publish his first works freely on the Internet. First only on Steemit and that also only with very moderate interest from the readers. Only a few weeks later, KiBLS found the platform Minds where, to his great surprise, he quickly found many subscribers and a steadily growing readership.

When the FB censorship wave hit Vietnam, Minds was flooded with new users from Vietnam and KiBLS realized that his poetry was not only popular in the German and English speaking countries, but also with Vietnamese readers. Through sites like Golos and Uptrennd (meanwhile offline), KiBLS realized that his poetry was finding many readers even in Russia and Africa.

Over time, KiBLS reached well over 8,000 subscribers on Minds alone and began to engage in various censorship-free social media. However, he soon realized that it was very time consuming to publish in many different medias and limited himself to the platform Minds where he already had 8,000 subscribers. This was followed by several articles from various online journals (for example in the "Searchenginejournal" and the Turkish "Teknotomy") in which KiBLS' channel and his poems were recommended.

Over time, the butterfly and the chessboard became a kind of trademark of KiBLS. Then, in 2021, KiBLS withdrew completely from social media and it became quieter on the internet around his poetry.

The PDF versions of the German e-books are still in progress. But the individual .txt files are already ready and can be downloaded under each poem. I am only one person and it is a lot of work to manage this site on my own.
