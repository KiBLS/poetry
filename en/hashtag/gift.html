Dear readers,

I have been running this poetry website for about a year now, and I have been sharing my poems with interested readers over many years on several social media platforms with magnificent feedbacks. My poems are very important to me and I want to share them with as many people as possible. Therefore, I can't sell them, as that would be counterproductive to the purpose and intent behind my poems.

I am also aware that these are difficult times and that even more difficult times lie ahead. I also don't want to take advantage of anyone or take away their hard earned income. I want everyone to feel good, confident and inspired when he reads and shares my poems, regardless of whether he can spare something or not.

However, if you can spare something, I would appreciate any gift, no matter how small, in appreciation of my work.

If you would like to do something additional to show me that you appreciate my poems, I would appreciate a thank you email maybe more than I would appreciate a financial gift. But I look forward to it if you can support me in any way. This would show me that my poems are being appreciated and have been read, and that I have made the right decision to share them with the world.

Thank you in advance for your support and for taking the time to visit my site and read my poems.

Sincerely

KiBLS

Email: kiblsfoo@disroot.org
