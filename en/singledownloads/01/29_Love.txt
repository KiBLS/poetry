Love

My true love is out out there somewhere,
I'd love to rush to her today!

I want to tell her everything, describing how I feel,
that her smile may flush all the bad out of me.

I want to give her everything, up to the last shirt,
because "true love" is no longer foreign to me.

(written by KiBLS)
