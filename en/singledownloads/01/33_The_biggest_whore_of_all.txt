The biggest whore of all

I love to play with her.
It's pure fun to abuse her.

Everyone wants to own and covet her,
but only a few will really feel her.

Easily she will pull you back.
And even easier you lose her for a lifetime.

She will take your dignity and your pride
And lets you then mourn and beg for it.

She wants to be abused. This whore ...
... I silenced her request because I desire her.

I love her and want to lose myself inside her.
I do not want to live without her anymore.

I can not strive without her.
This deep longing after her...
... the longing for my greatest treasure;

>>> "The power"

- The truly biggest whore of all time!

(written by KiBLS)
