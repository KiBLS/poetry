Me and you

I do not wear the same shirt.
And yet you are not foreign to me.

I do not wear the same pants.
And yet I like your pose.

I do not wear the same dress.
And yet I share your suffering.

I do not wear the same shoes.
And yet I am (as you)!

(written by KiBLS)
