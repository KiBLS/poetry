Increase the frequency

High vibrations can heal, would you agree?
In every free moment, I read only poetry!

I just start to reveal hidden mysteries!
So now I rise above all past miseries.

My mind is now changing its frequency,
in such a state I am most frequently.

On this way, the world is open to me.
Many would call that the secret key!

A new consciousness opens all borders!
It simply implement old sacred orders!

(written by KiBLS)
