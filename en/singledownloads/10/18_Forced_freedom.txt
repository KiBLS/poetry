Forced freedom

A new day is dawning.
The master is drawing.

Finally found the source.
Discovered the true force.

All thoughts begin to groove.
This makes the pen move.

The wisdom appears.
Just leaving all fears.

Mankind is ready to rise.
It's a spectacle for all eyes.

(written by KiBLS)
