Tuning of the Minds

We are united in our mind,
Our thoughts are perfectly aligned.

We act in concert as one!
We are only here to have fun.

We are never lost or separated!
We resonate with what's created.

Our energy is never ending.
Through the messages we are sending.

(written by KiBLS and Bart23)

Written: ~13.04.2020 | Published: 15.04.2020
