My new world

The world is my home.
On this earth I'll roam.

Where I am, I will live.
What I have, I can give.

What I need, I will obtain.
I make gifts from my brain.

I give away all my wisdom.
Thus I overcome any schism.

I unite myself with the whole.
That's how I achieve any goal.

(written KiBLS)
