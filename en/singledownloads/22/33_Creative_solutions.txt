Creative solutions

Creativity easily opens all doors.
The entire world can be yours.

Creative thinking is essential,
to unleash your full potential.

Every problem can be solved.
When creativity is involved.

Do not think in simple drawers!
For that is how you win by scores.

Go new ways and forget all the rules!
For this you already have all the tools!

(written KiBLS)
