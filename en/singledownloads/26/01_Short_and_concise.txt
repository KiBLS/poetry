Short and concise

Originated in Odin's forest.
A long time this was promised.

North of the Midnight Mountain.
Re-born the spiritual fountain.

Ready for the world to shine.
Ready to change water to wine.

The last awakening is imminent.
Acting like a nature immigrant.

Enigmatic lines will be revealed.
Earth becomes again a holy field.

Verses become again holy laws.
By eliminating the harmful cause.

(written by KiBLS)
