Graphene-Brain Interface

Graphene is in the air and in food.
It depresses your health and mood.

They take over the minds and brains.
Graphene pushes through the veins.

AI-Powered Graphene-Brain Interfaces.
When killing, they leave no traces.

Organic sulfur can help to detoxify,
otherwise you are controlled by AI.

An artificial bio robot under control,
this is the way of selling your soul.

In pumpkin seeds you can find zinc.
It suppresses your virtual uplink.

Acerola cherry is a vitamin C miracle.
It supports the body, it is empirical.

Now get all the toxic muck out of you.
Get to know yourself and what is true.
